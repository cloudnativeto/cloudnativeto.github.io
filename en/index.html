Establishment of the Cloud Native Community

Today the Community Steering Committee announced the official formation of the Cloud Native Community.

Cloud Native Community began with the founding of Kubernetes & CloudNative in 2016, the group covers thousands of early cloud native advocates. Building on this group, in May 2020, CNCF ambassadors and opinion leaders in the open source communities launched a new initiative to bring the Cloud Native Communtiy. The community has been upgraded to a cloud native community, aiming to build an open and inclusive communication environment to promote the spread and popularity of cloud native technology.
